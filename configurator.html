Solar Panel Configurator

Guide: Choose solar panels according to your requirements.

  • Budget Range: Low: $5,000 - $10,000, Moderate: $10,000 - $20,000, High: $20,000 - $30,000+
  • Electricity Bill: Moderate: $50 - $100 per month, 300 - 600 kWhr per month; High: $100 - $200+ per month, 600 - 1200+ kWhr per month
  • Energy Independence Goals: Choose 'Moderate' if you aim for gradual reduction, 'High' for a more substantial shift towards energy independence.
  • Roof Orientation and Shading: 'Ideal' is best for maximum sun exposure, 'Moderate' if your roof has some shading, 'Flexible' if your roof has varying orientations and shading conditions.
